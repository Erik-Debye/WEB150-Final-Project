Fresh and Contemporary Eats

This establishment aspires to create modern Thai and Pan-Asian dishes,
aiming to keep a focus on the traditional while still embracing new flavors.

Humble Beginings

AltThai was established in 2005 by Kevin C.,
who left his career as a machanical engineer to pursue and
share his love for food alongside his wife and brother.

AltThai strives to serve the community with a welcoming atmosphere and delectable eats. Hope to see you soon!

Great Food Requires Great Friends

Our recipes are derived from the very best Thai chefs in the world. Check out Pailin Chongchitnant's video for our Pad Thai Inspiration.
