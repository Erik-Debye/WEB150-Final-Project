Who We Are

AltThai is a locally owned and operated business in Arlington Heights, Illinois.

Established in 2005, AltThai was born quickly after owner Kevin C., saw an opportunity to share his Thai culture within a community that as of then lacked Thai and Pan-Asian eateries. Alongside his supportive wife and brother, he developed a menu of delights aiming to strike a balance between the traditional and the contemporary.

No matter when or who you are eating out with, AltThai pledges to work towards create the warmest and most welcoming atmospheres. We want to be your number one choice for that first date (and those thereafter), family night, or even just as a single take-away dish. We are continuing to expand our offerings & service and we hope to serve you soon!
